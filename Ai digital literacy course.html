01
Week One
Welcome to the Digital World 🌐
Foundations · Computer Basics · Digital Mindset
Session 01
Navigating Your Digital Workspace
🎯 Learning Objective

Students will confidently use file management systems, understand folder structures, and master keyboard shortcuts that save time every day.

⚡ Hands-On Activity
"Digital Desk Makeover" — Organise a messy mock folder system on Google Drive using a naming convention you design yourself. Race to find a hidden file the fastest!
Session 02
Google Workspace — Your New Best Friend
🎯 Learning Objective

Students will use Google Docs, Sheets, and Drive collaboratively — understanding real-time editing, sharing permissions, and comment features.

⚡ Hands-On Activity
"Class Wiki" — In teams, build a shared Google Doc with tips for new students. Each person edits simultaneously — watch the magic of live collaboration happen!
02
Week Two
Meet Your AI Assistant 🤖
Claude AI · Prompt Engineering · Smart Searching
Session 03
Talking to AI — The Art of the Perfect Prompt
🎯 Learning Objective

Students will understand how large language models work, write effective prompts using the Context–Task–Format framework, and evaluate AI responses critically.

⚡ Hands-On Activity
"Prompt Battle" — Given the same goal (e.g. "plan a study schedule"), each student writes a different prompt for Claude. Compare results and vote on the most useful output. Discuss: why did some prompts win?
Session 04
AI Ethics — Power, Bias & Responsibility
🎯 Learning Objective

Students will identify risks of AI misuse (misinformation, plagiarism, bias), understand academic integrity, and form personal guidelines for responsible AI use.

⚡ Hands-On Activity
"Spot the Fake" — Analyse 5 pieces of AI-generated content (text, images, headlines). Which is real? Which is fabricated? Build a class "AI Red Flags" checklist together.
03
Week Three
Design That Speaks 🎨
Canva · Visual Storytelling · AI Image Generation
Session 05
Canva Mastery — From Blank to Beautiful
🎯 Learning Objective

Students will apply design principles (alignment, contrast, hierarchy, white space) to create polished, professional visuals using Canva's tools and templates.

⚡ Hands-On Activity
"Rebrand a School Club" — Choose any school club and design a poster, social media post, and banner in Canva using a consistent brand palette. Present your identity system to the class.
Session 06
AI Image Generation — Imagination Meets Technology
🎯 Learning Objective

Students will use AI image tools (Adobe Firefly, Canva AI, or DALL·E) to generate images with descriptive prompts and discuss copyright, ownership, and attribution.

⚡ Hands-On Activity
"Illustrated Story Cover" — Write a 3-sentence story premise, then generate a book cover using an AI image tool. Refine your prompt at least 3 times. Final covers go in a class digital gallery!
04
Week Four
Present Like a Pro — Final Project 🚀
PowerPoint · Presentation Skills · Capstone Showcase
Session 07
Slides That Win — Professional Presentation Design
🎯 Learning Objective

Students will design slide decks using the "one idea per slide" rule, slide hierarchy, data visualisation, and speaker notes — turning raw information into compelling narratives.

⚡ Hands-On Activity
"The Makeover Challenge" — Each student receives a deliberately ugly, text-heavy slide deck. Using Canva or Google Slides, redesign it in 30 minutes. Before/after reveals are shared with the class.
Session 08
Capstone Showcase — You're the Expert Now!
🎯 Learning Objective

Students will demonstrate their combined skills by delivering a 5-minute presentation on a topic of their choice, using AI tools, professional slides, and original visuals.

⚡ Hands-On Activity
"Digital Future Pitch" — Each student presents: "How will AI change [my chosen field] in the next 10 years?" Judged on slide design, research quality, AI tool use, and delivery. Certificates awarded! 🏆

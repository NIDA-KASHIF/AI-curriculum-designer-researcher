03 — Methodology

How I Design Curricula

A 4-step AI-assisted research process that produces pedagogically sound, culturally relevant and deeply researched educational content.

🔍
Research & Synthesis
Deep dive into primary sources — classical texts, peer-reviewed literature and authentic hadith collections.
🏗️
Structure & Scaffold
Apply Bloom's Taxonomy, backward design and Islamic pedagogical frameworks to build progressive learning journeys.
✍️
Content Creation
Write detailed session plans, objectives, activities and assessments in Urdu, English or bilingual formats.
🔄
Review & Refine
Iterative quality review for accuracy, clarity, age-appropriateness and alignment with learning outcomes.
